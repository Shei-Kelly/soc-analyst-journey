# SOC Alerts and Triage

## What is an Alert?
A notification indicating potentially suspicious activity.

## True Positive
Alert correctly identifies malicious activity.

## False Positive
Alert appears suspicious but is actually legitimate.

## Severity Levels
- Low
- Medium
- High
- Critical

## Alert Triage
Process of validating and prioritizing alerts.

## Investigation Questions
- Who?
- What?
- When?
- Where?
- Why?

## Key Takeaway
Alerts are starting points for investigations, not proof of an attack.
