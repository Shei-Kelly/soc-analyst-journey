# Day 5 – Hour 1: Introduction to Threat Hunting

## What is Threat Hunting?

Threat hunting is the proactive search for malicious activity that may have bypassed security controls.

## SOC Analyst vs Threat Hunter

SOC Analyst:
- Responds to alerts
- Reactive

Threat Hunter:
- Searches proactively
- Hypothesis-driven

## IOC vs IOA

IOC:
- Evidence of compromise
- Malicious IPs
- Malicious domains
- Malware hashes

IOA:
- Suspicious behavior
- Failed logins
- PowerShell execution
- Lateral movement

## Key Insight

Threat hunters search for suspicious activity even when no alert exists.
