## Key Lessons Learned

- SIEMs automate log collection and correlation.
- Alerts are indicators, not proof of attacks.
- Investigations require supporting evidence.
- MITRE ATT&CK helps classify attacker behavior.
- AI can accelerate investigations but must be validated.
- Documentation is a critical SOC skill.
